What Is hackX?

The first edition of ECX hackathon themed hackX "Building Solutions For The Future" focuses on building innovative solutions to the following sectors: Health, Real Estate, Logistics, Education. The competition enables programmers to share skills in teams solving a real engineering challenge.

Who Is Eligible?

The challenge is open to Nigerian undergraduates in selected universities and colleges. There would be round(s) of shortlisting after the open application and review of qualification by special panelists.
● Participants must be a student of any of the underlisted universities or colleges:
  • — YABA COLLEGE OF TECHNOLOGY
  • — UNIVERSITY OF IBADAN
  • — OBAFEMI AWOLOWO UNIVERSITY
  • — UNIVERSITY OF LAGOS
  • — UNIVERSITY OF ILORIN
  • — COVENANT UNIVERSITY
  • — BABCOCK/BELLS UNIVERSITY
  • — IBADAN POLYTECHNIC
  • — FEDERAL UNIVERSITY OF TECHNOLOGY, AKURE
  • — FEDERAL UNIVERSITY OF AGRICULTURE, ABEOKUTA
  • — KWARA STATE UNIVERSITY
  • — LAGOS STATE UNIVERSITY
  • — ADEKUNLE AJASIN UNIVERSITY, AKUNGBA
● Participants must have constant access to the internet, and a good laptop, during the period of the hackathon


CRITERIA FOR SELECTION

  • Selection would be aimed at giving equal gender opportunities.
  • Participants must be active on Slack, for ease of communication.
  • Each team would be made up of 4 individuals
    •
  • The hackathon is only opened to undergraduates of qualified colleges as stated.
  • The admin would select based on the merit discretion of qualified candidates.
  • There are no limits to the number of students from any university or college
